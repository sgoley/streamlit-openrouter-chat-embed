## ARS / Advanced Recovery Systems

**Jan 2017 - Oct 2018**

### Business and Data Analyst

* BI / KPI Dashboards for 100-user, $300K/day call center
* Salesforce custom reporting through SOQL, Wave Analytics with custom JSON reporting structures and interactive dashboards
* Salesforce Embedded PowerBI reports using Azure Active Directory services and PowerBI Desktop
* Engineered Chatfuel Chat AI integration for location-based service responses with REST callouts through Integromat
* Integrated multiple relational and non-relational databases (SQL, Postgres, MongoDB, Cache)
* Ad-hoc analysis reports in Excel and R for process improvements, timeline analysis, customer segmentation, and call center efficiency

### Salesforce Admin

* System administrator for Salesforce.com environment with 150+ users
* Force.com development: Apex classes, triggers, components, VisualForce pages, SOQL and SOSL queries
* Salesforce data security, data models, and data migration
* Proof-of-concept development with product and business users
* VisualForce development and Salesforce data tools (Data Loader, Excel Connector, etc.)
* Support and administration for NewVoiceMedia IVR System
* Backup procedures and emergency rerouting for IVR system

### Commentary

I took this position as my first step towards programming as a career. I was the primary data analyst for the marketing group responsible for analytics of the call center, business development group, and paid & organic marketing teams. Eventually, due to significant responsibilities and technical overlap, I served as part of the Salesforce platform team since it was the primary CDP and attribution platform. I introduced custom analytics using R and [Shiny](https://shiny.posit.co) for more customized and interactive dashboards across groups. I was the technical IC on our evaluation of CRM Analytics (formerly Einstein Analytics) to integrate analytics more natively into Salesforce. We eventually moved towards PowerBI for unified company-wide analytics. During my time at ARS, I worked on diverse projects including data integration, chatbots, and marketing attribution strategies. I left to pursue an opportunity at a fintech startup closer to my interests in finance and technology.
