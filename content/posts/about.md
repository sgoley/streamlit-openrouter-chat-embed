# About Me

Welcome to the personal website of Scott Goley! I'm a data and analytics engineer with a passion for building tools that make data more accessible and actionable. With a background in psychology, statistics, and startups, I feel I am uniquely positioned to bridge the gap between pure data engineering and diverse experiences in finance and other domains. I have a particular interest in AI due to its inspiration from human cognition and neurobiology.

I use this space to share notes, observations, and project updates.

* Discussions of industry trends and best practices
* Tactical advice for data professionals
* Practical notes from side projects
* Post-mortems and lessons learned
